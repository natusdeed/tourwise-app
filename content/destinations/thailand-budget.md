# Thailand - Budget Travel Paradise

Thailand offers incredible value for budget travelers, with beautiful beaches, rich culture, delicious food, and affordable accommodations throughout the country.

## Why Visit Thailand?

Thailand is one of the world's best budget destinations, offering:
- Affordable accommodations ($10-30 per night)
- Delicious and cheap street food ($1-3 per meal)
- Beautiful beaches and islands
- Rich cultural experiences
- Friendly locals and easy travel

## Budget Accommodations

- **Hostels**: $5-15 per night
- **Guesthouses**: $15-30 per night
- **Budget hotels**: $20-50 per night
- **Beach bungalows**: $10-40 per night

## Budget Food

- **Street food**: $1-3 per meal
- **Local restaurants**: $3-8 per meal
- **Western food**: $5-15 per meal
- **Markets**: Great for fresh produce and snacks

## Getting Around

- **Buses**: Very affordable, extensive network
- **Trains**: Comfortable and cheap
- **Domestic flights**: Budget airlines available
- **Tuk-tuks**: Negotiate prices before riding
